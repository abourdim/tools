What is this app?

A Workshop-DIY educational web app. Explore, create, and innovate!

How do I change the theme?

Open Settings (⚙️) and pick a theme from the dropdown.

How do I change the language?

Open Settings (⚙️) and pick your language. Arabic enables RTL automatically.

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser. No data is sent anywhere.

🎨 Themes

8 built-in themes: 6 dark (Mosque, Zellige, Andalus, Space, Jungle, Robot) and 2 light Islamic themes (Riad, Medina).

🌐 Languages

Trilingual support: English, Français, العربية. Arabic automatically enables right-to-left layout.

📜 Activity Log

Timestamped, color-coded log. Clear or copy to clipboard. Types: info, success, error, TX, RX.

🔒 Privacy

Local-first, privacy-first. All data stays in your browser. No tracking, no analytics, no external calls.
